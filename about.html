About Our Unani Clinic

Dr Muzammil’s Unani Clinic is a famous alternative care Clinic in south India, young renowned Unani physician Dr. Muzammil is the chief consultant of the clinic. Under his guidance, well-trained Unani physicians and therapists serve the patients to treat various acute and chronic disease with applying all the therapeutic tools in the Unani medicine.

Unani or unanipathy is practiced by diagnosing the patient through various examinations like checking the pulse, conducting urine and blood test. As unanipathy deals with the balance in the internal, external surroundings and ecological system of a person, it deals with not only curing and healing the body disease, but also focuses on restoring the complete health condition of the person.

We treat our patients with equal care by following the Unani system of medicine, so that they live healthy and happy lives. Unani System is part of the knowledge of indigenous cultures, which has stood the test of time. . The system has shown remarkable results in curing ailments of nervous system, skin and hair, circulatory system and several other acute and chronic diseases. Now the system has transcended national boundaries and is popular among the masses globally
